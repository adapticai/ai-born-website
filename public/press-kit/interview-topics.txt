AI-BORN: SUGGESTED INTERVIEW TOPICS

Author: Mehran Granfar
Book: AI-Born: The Machine Core, the Human Cortex, and the Next Economy of Being

OVERVIEW

Mehran Granfar is available for interviews on AI transformation, organisational design,
autonomous agents, and the future of work. Below are suggested topics and talking points.

---

CORE THEMES

1. THE AI-NATIVE TRANSFORMATION
   • Why traditional organisations can't simply add AI tools
   • The difference between AI-enabled and AI-born
   • How three people can now orchestrate what once took 30,000
   • Real-world examples of AI-native operations

2. THE FIVE PLANES ARCHITECTURE
   • Complete framework for designing AI-born organisations
   • How Data, Models, Agents, Orchestration, and Governance layers work together
   • Common mistakes in AI transformation
   • How to start the journey from AI-enabled to AI-native

3. REDEFINING HUMAN ROLES
   • The New Triumvirate: Architect, Intent-Setter, Guardian
   • What happens to middle management in AI-native organisations?
   • Why judgement, taste, and intent become more valuable (not less)
   • Reskilling strategies for the AI era

4. COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE IN THE AI ERA
   • The Defensibility Stack: where moats live now
   • Why governance and trust matter more than scale
   • Iteration half-life and speed of adaptation
   • How architecture becomes competitive advantage

5. PRACTICAL TRANSFORMATION
   • The Cognitive Overhead Index: diagnosing institutional drag
   • Where to start your AI transformation journey
   • Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
   • Timeline expectations for AI-native transition

6. GOVERNANCE AND ETHICS
   • Trust rails for autonomous systems
   • Who is accountable when AI agents make decisions?
   • Ethical guardrails without stifling innovation
   • Balancing autonomy with oversight

7. THE FUTURE OF WORK
   • Beyond labour and capital: new models of value creation
   • Portable benefits and participation dividends
   • Why job titles are becoming obsolete
   • Human purpose in a post-labour economy

8. ECONOMIC AND SOCIETAL IMPLICATIONS
   • How AI-born organisations reshape markets
   • Distribution vs. consolidation in the AI era
   • Policy implications for regulators
   • Long-term scenarios for AI and human flourishing
